1. Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO)

Dexton Solvexx provides enterprise-grade Recruitment Process Outsourcing (RPO) solutions designed to manage your entire end-to-end recruitment lifecycle. We deploy dedicated recruiting infrastructure, modern applicant tracking tools, and experienced sourcing specialists to optimize key metrics. From initial requirement drafting and target marketing to deep vetting assessments, structured interview scheduling, and global payroll compliance setups, we assume complete ownership of the hiring pipeline.

Strategic Sourcing & Vetting Capabilities:

  • Dedicated senior recruitment specialists aligned to your exact hours and timezone.
  • Continuous target market mapping and pipeline generation for hard-to-fill and niche technical slots.
  • Advanced ATS configuration, automated resumes vetting, and digital screening workflows.

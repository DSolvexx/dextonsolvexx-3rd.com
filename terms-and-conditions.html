3. Commercial Invoicing, Payment Terms, & EOR Structures

Corporate clients engaging Dexton Solvexx for offshore remote staff or RPO services agree to adhere to strict billing and payment schedules:

  • Consolidated Monthly Invoicing: Clients receive a single, consolidated monthly business invoice in their domestic currency (USD, GBP, AUD, EUR, etc.), covering remote payroll, local compliance, benefits administration, and Dexton Solvexx service fees.
  • Net Payment Terms: All invoices are payable under strict **Net 14** or **Net 30** terms from the date of invoice generation, unless otherwise specified in the client MSA.
  • Late Payment Liabilities: Late payments are subject to service charge interest at the rate of 1.5% per month, or the maximum allowable rate under local commercial laws, whichever is lower.
  • Currency Valuations: To protect operations from foreign exchange shifts, invoices are adjusted in alignment with standard interbank exchange rates at the time of payroll calculations.

Dexton Solvexx acts as the Employer of Record (EOR) or contracting agency for placed offshore talent. We maintain strict legal contracts with all remote professionals, ensuring that **100% of all intellectual property rights**, including source code, design mockups, clinical data, patent documentation, and database rights created during their service tenure, are owned by the client company.

This complete assignment of IP rights takes effect automatically upon **full and final payment** of all associated Dexton Solvexx invoices. If the client defaults on payments, Dexton Solvexx retains a lien on all work product generated by the remote professionals during the default window until all outstanding balances are cleared.

5. Strict Non-Solicitation & Circumvention Protections

Building, sifting, and maintaining a vetted global talent database requires massive capital and operational investments from Dexton Solvexx. Therefore, we protect our business model with strict non-solicitation covenants:

Clients agree not to solicit, recruit, or directly hire any remote professional introduced or placed by Dexton Solvexx outside of our platform, whether as a direct employee, independent contractor, or through a third-party agency. If a client circumvents Dexton Solvexx to directly hire a placed professional, they agree to pay a standard **Direct Placement Transfer Fee** equal to 30% of the candidate's projected annual salary or US$30,000 (whichever is higher) as liquidated damages to cover sourcing operations.

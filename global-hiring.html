Global Hiring Solutions

Scale smoothly in new international corridors. We manage localized payroll, direct EOR filings, and tax compliance, shielding your business from liability.

1. Navigating Cross-Border Hiring: The EOR Model

Expanding your talent operations internationally brings massive growth potential, but it also introduces complex administrative challenges. Navigating differing cross-border labor laws, setting up localized legal entities, managing international payroll, and staying compliant with regional tax systems can create significant friction and business liability.

Dexton Solvexx's **Global Hiring Solutions** and **Employer of Record (EOR)** services handle all of these operational complexities for you. We operate as the legal employer of your remote staff in their home countries, managing payroll, benefits, and tax compliance, while they work directly under your management. This allows you to scale global divisions smoothly without the overhead of establishing local physical entities.

2. Core Structural Benefits of Our Global Hiring Solutions

Deploying your international remote teams through Dexton Solvexx EOR ensures absolute compliance and operational ease:

  • Zero Local Entity Overhead: Hire legally in over 30 countries without the high cost and complexity of setting up localized corporate branches.
  • Complete Compliance Shielding: We take full legal responsibility for local labor rules, GDPR data guidelines, and regional IP protections.
  • Consolidated Payroll Administration: Receive a single monthly business invoice in your local currency, covering global payroll, benefits, and local taxes.

3. Comprehensive EOR Infrastructure

Our global hiring solutions manage all administrative operations:

  • Localized Payroll & Taxes: Processing salaries, computing required tax deductions, and filing regional social contributions.
  • Standard Benefits Administration: Managing health insurance plans, pension filings, statutory leaves, and local holidays.
  • IP Covenants Protection: Securing 100% intellectual property assignment to your enterprise upon invoice clearance.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between an EOR and an independent contractor model?

Under an EOR setup, remote staff are legally employed by Dexton Solvexx under regional labor laws, eliminating the business risks of contractor misclassification and audit liabilities.

Q: In what countries does Dexton Solvexx offer EOR support?

We manage comprehensive EOR operations across primary sourcing talent corridors in India, Singapore, Poland, Romania, Mexico, Colombia, and Brazil.
